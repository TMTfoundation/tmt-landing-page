Mock Trial Tournaments

The TMT Foundation is proud to support UCSD Mock Trial's participation in regional, invitational, and national tournaments throughout the year. Competing across the country builds advocacy skills, confidence, and camaraderie among students.

Our foundation also helps host tournaments at UC San Diego, welcoming teams from across the nation and giving students a chance to shine on their home campus. Details about upcoming tournaments will be posted here and on our calendar.
