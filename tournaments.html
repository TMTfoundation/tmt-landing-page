Tournaments

The Triton Mock Trial Foundation proudly sponsors UCSD’s participation in competitive collegiate mock trial tournaments across the nation.

Each year, students compete at invitationals, regionals, and national-level events—showcasing advocacy, professionalism, and sportsmanship. Support helps cover travel, registration, and training expenses.
