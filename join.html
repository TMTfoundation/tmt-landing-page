Join Our Network

Whether you’re an alum, a parent, or a friend of the program, joining the TMT Foundation network is a great way to stay involved. By becoming a member, you’ll receive updates about UCSD Mock Trial, opportunities to mentor students, and invitations to special events.

To join our mailing list or learn more, please contact us directly at info@tmtfoundation.org.
