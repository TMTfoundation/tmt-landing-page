Foundation Committees

The TMT Foundation operates through several dedicated committees that ensure the continued success of UCSD Mock Trial. Each committee is composed of alumni and community members who lend their time and expertise.

Fundraising Committee: Coordinates campaigns, donor outreach, and sponsorship opportunities.

Events Committee: Plans alumni events, networking nights, and social gatherings.

Alumni Relations Committee: Maintains engagement with past team members and builds alumni networks.

Coaching Advisory Committee: Provides guidance to the coaching staff and helps support program development.
