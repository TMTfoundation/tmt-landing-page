About the TMT Foundation

The Triton Mock Trial Foundation was created to support the nationally ranked UC San Diego Mock Trial program. Founded by alumni and supported by the community, we work to ensure long-term sustainability for the program through financial support, alumni mentorship, and community engagement.

Our foundation provides funding for tournament travel, coaching, and competitive preparation. We also organize events and initiatives that keep alumni involved and students inspired.

Whether you’re an alum, parent, or supporter of the legal advocacy community, we welcome your involvement and thank you for your commitment to future generations of student advocates.
