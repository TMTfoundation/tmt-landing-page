About the Foundation

The Triton Mock Trial Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting the undergraduate mock trial program at UC San Diego. Our mission is to build long-term sustainability for the team by providing mentorship, funding, alumni networking, and strategic guidance.

Founded by alumni and supporters of UCSD Mock Trial, the foundation works to ensure future generations of Triton advocates have the resources they need to thrive in the courtroom and beyond.
