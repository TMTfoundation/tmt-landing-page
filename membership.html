Membership

Members of the Triton Mock Trial Foundation are a vital part of our growing alumni and supporter network. Whether you're a former competitor, coach, or parent, your involvement strengthens the future of UCSD Mock Trial.

Benefits of membership include newsletters, alumni networking opportunities, early invites to events, and the chance to mentor or support current students. There are no dues—just a shared commitment to advocacy, excellence, and access.

To join, visit the Join page or email us at info@tmtfoundation.org.
