Membership

Membership in the Triton Mock Trial Foundation is open to all supporters who believe in the value of undergraduate mock trial. Members receive quarterly updates, invitations to alumni events, and opportunities to connect with students and fellow alumni.

Joining the Foundation is a meaningful way to stay involved and shape the future of UCSD Mock Trial. Whether you’re a recent graduate or a long-time supporter, we welcome you.
