Membership

Becoming a member of the Triton Mock Trial Foundation connects you to a community of alumni, legal professionals, and advocates who support the growth of UCSD Mock Trial.

Members receive updates, invitations to exclusive events, and opportunities to engage with the next generation of competitors.

Membership is open to all supporters. Join us in advancing advocacy education at UCSD.
